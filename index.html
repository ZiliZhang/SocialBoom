HOW OUR SYSTEM WORKS

Everything is very simple! Each guest will tell his/her friends about your institution

WHAT'S THE TRICK?

Advertising at each connection

Advertising will be 100% conveyed by customers. Clients will see an ad of your institution and are required to login via social media to access the Internet.

Guests advertise to their friends

When a guest connect to the Internet, automatically published a post on the page of the client in the social media with your advertising. Thus,clients advertise for you among their friends.

Detailed statistics

You will be provided with detailed statistics for advertising and connected guests, sorting by date and type of social network.
